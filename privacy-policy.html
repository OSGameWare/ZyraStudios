Privacy Policy

Zyra Studios Privacy Policy
Last Updated: January 13, 2026

Welcome to Zyra Studios. This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile games and services developed and published by us. We believe in total transparency and minimal data collection to ensure a safe gaming environment.

1. Information Collection & Use

Zyra Studios does not directly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not ask for, nor do we have access to, your name, email address, physical address, or phone number. Our games are designed to be played without the need for user account registration.

2. Automated Data Collection (Third-Party Services)

To improve game stability and performance, our applications use third-party service providers that may automatically collect certain non-personal technical information. This data is used solely for diagnostic purposes. These services include:

  • Google Play Services: Provides game functionality such as achievements and cloud saves.
  • Google Analytics & Android Vitals: Collects anonymized technical data, including device model, OS version, and crash logs to help us identify and fix bugs.
  • Device Identifiers: Google may use anonymized identifiers (such as an Advertising ID) to track installs and provide secure services.

You can review Google’s Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Data Security & Retention

Any technical data handled by Google is encrypted in transit (using HTTPS). Zyra Studios does not maintain a database of user information. Because we do not store personal data, we have no means of identifying individual users; however, users can manage their Google Play data directly through their Google Account settings.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our games are intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
